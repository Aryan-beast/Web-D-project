JIM CORBETT NATIONAL PARK

  • Location: Nainital, ‎Uttarakhand‎, ‎India‎
  • Area :520 km2 (200.8 sq mi)
  • Established: 1936
    •
Jim Corbett National Park is part of the Corbett Tiger Reserve, a Project Tiger Reserve in Uttarakhand's Nainital district. The tiger-rich Corbett landscape is well-known and fabled. Corbett Nature Reserve is India's oldest and most prestigious National Park. It was also the birthplace of Project Tiger in 1973. To protect the most endangered species and the Royal of India called Tigers, this unique tiger territory is known as Project Tiger's father.
