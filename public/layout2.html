KAZIRANGA NATIONAL PARK

  • Location: Kanchanjuri, ‎Assam‎, ‎India‎
  • Area :430 km2 (166 sq mi)
  • Established: 1908
    •
Kaziranga National Park, with its elephant-grass meadows, swampy lagoons, and dense forests, is home to over 2200 Indian one-horned rhinoceros, roughly 2/3 of the world population. UNESCO designated the park a World Heritage Site in 1985. The park's vast expanse of tall elephant grass, marshland, and dense tropical moist broad leaf forests is beautiful, but the presence of the Brahmaputra river is mysterious.
