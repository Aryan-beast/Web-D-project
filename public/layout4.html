PERIYAR NATIONAL PARK

  • Location: Districts of Idukki and Pathanamthitta, ‎Kerala‎, ‎India‎
  • Area 305 km2 (118 sq mi)
  • Established: 1982
    •
The Periyar National Park and Wildlife Sanctuary is a protected area in the Indian state of Kerala that is located in the districts of Idukki and Pathanamthitta. It is notable for having both an elephant reserve and a tiger reserve within its borders. The park is a haven for rare, endemic, and endangered species of flora and fauna, and it serves as the primary watershed for two of Kerala's most important rivers, the Periyar and the Pamba.
